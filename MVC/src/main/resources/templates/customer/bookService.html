Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

You can schedule a service appointment in multiple ways:

  • Use our online booking system on this website
  • Call our customer service at +91 98765 43210
  • Visit our service center in person
  • Use the Albany mobile app

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to ensure availability for your preferred time slot.

At Albany, we service a wide range of vehicles including:

  • Passenger cars of all makes and models
  • SUVs and crossovers
  • Luxury vehicles
  • Light commercial vehicles

Our technicians are certified and trained to work on both domestic and imported vehicles, ensuring high-quality service regardless of your vehicle type.

Service duration depends on the type of service your vehicle needs:

  • Regular maintenance: 1-2 hours
  • Oil change and filter replacement: 30-45 minutes
  • Comprehensive service: 3-4 hours
  • Major repairs: May take 1-2 days depending on complexity

We provide estimated completion times when you book your service, and you can track the progress in real-time through our customer portal.
